# Opportunity Scoring Framework v1

## Purpose

The Opportunity Scoring Framework evaluates business opportunities using a standardized scoring model.

The objective is not prediction.

The objective is structured decision support.

---

## Scoring Categories

### 1. Opportunity Score

Measures overall attractiveness.

Range:

1 - 10

Factors:

- Market demand
- Growth potential
- Sustainability
- Accessibility

---

### 2. Competition Score

Measures competitive pressure.

Range:

1 - 10

Low score:

Limited competition.

High score:

Crowded market.

---

### 3. Startup Difficulty

Measures implementation difficulty.

Range:

1 - 10

Factors:

- Technical complexity
- Required skills
- Capital requirements
- Operational complexity

---

### 4. Time To Revenue

Measures expected speed to first revenue.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Fast path to revenue.

Low score:

Long path to revenue.

---

### 5. Monetization Potential

Measures income potential.

Range:

1 - 10

Factors:

- Pricing power
- Scalability
- Recurring revenue
- Expansion opportunities

---

## Example

Opportunity:

AI Automation Agency

Output:

Opportunity Score: 8

Competition Score: 7

Startup Difficulty: 6

Time To Revenue: 8

Monetization Potential: 9
