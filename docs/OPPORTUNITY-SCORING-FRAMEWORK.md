# Opportunity Scoring Framework v3

## Purpose

The Opportunity Scoring Framework evaluates business opportunities using a standardized scoring model.

The objective is not prediction.

The objective is structured decision support.

---

## Core Principle

Opportunity Score is not an evaluation category.

Opportunity Score is the final result produced by the framework.

---

## Evaluation Categories

### 1. Competition Score

Measures competitive pressure.

Range:

1 - 10

Low score:

Crowded market.

High score:

Limited competition.

---

### 2. Startup Difficulty

Measures how difficult it is to launch the opportunity.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Easy to launch.

Low score:

Difficult to launch.

Factors:

- Technical complexity
- Required skills
- Capital requirements
- Regulatory requirements

---

### 3. Fulfillment Difficulty

Measures how difficult it is to deliver results after the first customer is acquired.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Easy fulfillment.

Low score:

Difficult fulfillment.

Factors:

- Operational workload
- Service delivery complexity
- Support requirements
- Ongoing management

---

### 4. Time To Revenue

Measures speed to first meaningful revenue.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Fast revenue.

Low score:

Slow revenue.

Factors:

- Build time
- Sales cycle
- Customer onboarding

---

### 5. Economic Potential

Measures long-term economic value.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Strong economic potential.

Low score:

Weak economic potential.

Factors:

- Revenue potential
- Recurring revenue
- Scalability
- Asset value
- Exit potential

---

### 6. Distribution Difficulty

Measures customer acquisition difficulty.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Easy distribution.

Low score:

Difficult distribution.

Factors:

- Audience accessibility
- Marketing complexity
- Sales requirements
- Advertising costs

---

### 7. Testability

Measures how easily the opportunity can be validated.

Range:

1 - 10

High score:

Fast and inexpensive validation.

Low score:

Expensive or slow validation.

Factors:

- MVP complexity
- Validation cost
- Feedback speed
- Ease of experimentation

---

## Opportunity Score

Opportunity Score is generated from all framework categories.

Range:

1 - 100

Purpose:

Provide a single high-level assessment.

The detailed category scores must always remain visible.

---

## Future Metrics
