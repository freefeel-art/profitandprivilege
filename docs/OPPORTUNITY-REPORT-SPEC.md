# Opportunity Report Specification

## Purpose

Transform evaluation scores into actionable decision support.

The report is the primary output of the Opportunity Validator.

Users should leave with:

- Understanding
- Context
- Risks
- Next actions

The report must support decision making.

---

# Report Structure

## 1. Opportunity Summary

Display:

- Opportunity Name
- Opportunity Score
- Evaluation Date

Example:

Opportunity:

Micro SaaS

Opportunity Score:

64 / 100

---

## 2. Category Scores

Display all evaluation categories.

Example:

Competition Score:
4

Startup Difficulty:
3

Fulfillment Difficulty:
8

Time To Revenue:
4

Economic Potential:
10

Distribution Difficulty:
4

Testability:
5

---

## 3. Strengths

Identify the strongest aspects of the opportunity.

Example:

Strengths:

- High recurring revenue potential
- Strong scalability
- High asset value
- Attractive exit potential

---

## 4. Risks

Identify the most important risks.

Example:

Risks:

- Technical development requirements
- Competitive market
- Difficult customer acquisition

---

## 5. Interpretation

Translate scores into plain language.

Example:

This opportunity offers strong long-term economic potential.

The primary challenge is customer acquisition and product development.

The opportunity is suitable for builders willing to invest time before revenue.

---

## 6. Recommended Actions

Provide practical next steps.

Example:

1. Select a specific niche.
2. Validate demand.
3. Build a landing page.
4. Collect user feedback.
5. Develop a minimum viable product.

---

## 7. Verdict

Provide a concise recommendation.

Possible outcomes:

- Strong Opportunity
- Viable Opportunity
- Weak Opportunity
- Poor Opportunity

Example:

Verdict:

Strong Opportunity

Recommended for further validation.

---

# Design Rules

The report must never present scores without explanation.

Every score should lead to:

- insight
- context
- action

---

# MVP Scope

The MVP report includes:

- Summary
- Scores
- Strengths
- Risks
- Interpretation
- Recommended Actions
- Verdict

No charts.

No dashboards.

No historical tracking.
