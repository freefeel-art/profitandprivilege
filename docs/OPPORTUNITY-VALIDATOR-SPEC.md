# Opportunity Validator MVP Specification

## Purpose

The Opportunity Validator is the first functional product of ProfitAndPrivilege.

Its purpose is to help users quickly evaluate business opportunities using a structured scoring framework.

---

## User Input

The user provides:

- Business idea
- Opportunity
- Income stream
- Business model

Examples:

- AI Agency
- Affiliate Marketing
- Local Directory
- YouTube Channel
- SaaS Business
- Consulting Business

---

## Evaluation Engine

The validator evaluates:

1. Opportunity Score
2. Competition Score
3. Startup Difficulty
4. Time To Revenue
5. Monetization Potential

---

## Output

The user receives:

### Opportunity Score

Overall attractiveness.

### Competition Score

Competitive pressure.

### Startup Difficulty

Difficulty of implementation.

### Time To Revenue

Estimated speed to first revenue.

### Monetization Potential

Income potential.

---

## Recommendation

The system generates:

- Key strengths
- Key risks
- Recommended next actions

---

## MVP Rules

No accounts.

No login.

No membership.

No payment system.

No dashboard.

No database.

No AI agents.

The MVP validates the concept only.

---

## Success Criteria

A user can:

1. Enter an opportunity.
2. Receive an evaluation.
3. Understand strengths and risks.
4. Decide whether to investigate further.
