# Scoring Engine

## Purpose

Transform evaluation metrics into a final Opportunity Score.

The scoring engine must be transparent, explainable and reproducible.

---

## Input Metrics

1. Competition Score
2. Startup Difficulty
3. Time To Revenue
4. Monetization Potential
5. Distribution Difficulty
6. Testability

Range:

1 - 10

---

## Positive Factors

Higher is better:

- Time To Revenue
- Monetization Potential
- Testability

---

## Negative Factors

Lower is better:

- Competition Score
- Startup Difficulty
- Distribution Difficulty

---

## Weighting

Competition Score:
15%

Startup Difficulty:
15%

Time To Revenue:
20%

Monetization Potential:
25%

Distribution Difficulty:
15%

Testability:
10%

Total:
100%

---

## Opportunity Score

Range:

1 - 100

Purpose:

Provide a single summary score.

The detailed category scores always remain visible.

---

## Interpretation

90-100

Exceptional Opportunity

---

75-89

Strong Opportunity

---

60-74

Viable Opportunity

---

40-59

Weak Opportunity

---

Below 40

Poor Opportunity

---

## Rule

Users must never see only the Opportunity Score.

Users must always see:

- Individual scores
- Strengths
- Risks
- Recommended actions

The score is guidance.

Not a decision.
